Captain's Manual

Ship Stats Guide

  • ATK (Attack): Increases the damage output of your laser cannons.
  • DEF (Defense): Reduces the damage you take from enemy counter-attacks.
  • HP (Health): Your ship's durability. If HP reaches 0, you lose 10% Gold!
  • SPD (Speed): Essential for navigation and evasion.
  • CRIT (Critical): The percentage chance to land a devastating double-damage strike.

Core Facilities

  • MAP: Hunt space monsters to earn Gold, XP, and random item drops. Each attack costs 5 Stamina.
  • BAG (Inventory): Equip items to boost stats. Dismantle unwanted gear into Iron Ore & Dark Energy. Use the Factory to forge weapons!
  • SHOP: Exchange SOL for Gold, roll Black Market Gacha, or refill your ship's stamina.
